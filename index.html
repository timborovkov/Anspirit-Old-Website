What do you want?

Have you ever been in this situation when you are coming home tired and want some coffee or sleep or both? Q can communicate with the user and ask it about its regards. If the user wants something, Q will do its best to make it real! Let’s imagine a user is a big fan of Justin Bieber and he will have a concert in the user’s city soon. Q will tell about this to the user and ask him if he wants to go! Also, Q will check if a user’s friends are going as well.

Assistant and friend

Q is perfect to have in your office, or at home. If you have a question on your mind, Q will tell you the answer. You can talk with Q and it will respond to you. You can also set smart alarms, notifications and calendar events with Q. All these things are synced with cloud and there is no need to worry that you will not be reminded!

Open Source

Open Source is nowadays future and Q is part of Open Source. Anyone can develop extensions for Q or make their own version of Q. We have the marketplace for users to extend features of their Q and for developers to make some money out of it or make the extension for free! Q is fully written on node.js, it is the best programming language ever.

Home Automation

What if all the devices in your life could connect to the Internet? Not just computers and smartphones, but everything: clocks, garage doors, speakers, lights, doors, windows, window blinds, door bells, hot water heaters, appliances, you name it. And what if those devices could all communicate, send you information, and take your commands? It's not science fiction; it's the IoT, and it's a key component of home automation.

Friend

Q is always listening to you. It is the perfect friend for you and your family. If you have a question, just ask Q. You are working hard and doesn't have time for useless things like asking stupid questions in Google. Well, sometimes you just need to know "Who is Taylor Swift?" and now you can simply ask. With todays technology it is so native, like talking with real humans, but without real body.

Emotion Recognition

People’s emotions and moods are changing during a day and every person has their own personality. Q communicates with the user during a day and asks about its feelings through a little set of questions. That’s why Q can learn user’s emotions and thus can set the atmosphere of the home that fits perfectly with the user’s mood. If answering the questions is too much, we build also emotion recognition based on the facial expression of the user using camera, microphone and cool user interaction systems, like Facebook status updates, tweets etc.
